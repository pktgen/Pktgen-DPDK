Pktgen-DPDK - Traffic Generator powered by DPDK
=====================================================

**Pktgen-DPDK is a traffic generator powered by DPDK at wire rate traffic with 64 byte frames.**

** (Pktgen) Sounds like 'Packet-Gen'**
